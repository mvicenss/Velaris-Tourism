Rhysand

Rhysand is the charming High Lord of the Night Court. He rules from the House of Wind above the city and also maintains the Town House and River House in Velaris. Rhysand is the visionary who protects Velaris and its people.

Feyre Archeron

Once a mortal huntress and now the High Lady, Feyre is a celebrated painter. She lives with Rhysand and helped design the River House. Visitors may catch glimpses of her art at galleries or private gatherings.

Cassian

The Illyrian general of Velaris. He is known for training new warriors on the city's outskirts and for social gatherings at Rita's. His presence signals the security of Velaris.

Azriel

The enigmatic spymaster who keeps Velaris safe from unseen threats. Azriel is often seen in the city's darker corners or at discreet meetings; his loyalty to Rhysand and Feyre is unwavering.

Morrigan (Mor)

Rhysand's cousin and Third-in-Command. Mor oversees Velaris (while publicly managing the Court of Nightmares facade) and is a beloved figure in the city. She is often seen enjoying Velaris' nightlife, especially Rita's dancehall.

Amren

The mysterious Second-in-Command. Ancient and unique, Amren provides counsel and was instrumental in defending Velaris during the war. She lives in a private city apartment.

Nesta Archeron

Formidable warrior of the House of Wind and leader of the new Valkyrie unit. Though she often shuns social functions, citizens admire her fierce independence and loyalty.

Elain Archeron

Gentle gardener of the River House. Known as the kind, “flower-like” sister with the gift of second sight, Elain tends the city's revived flower gardens and represents a serene strength within the Night Court.
