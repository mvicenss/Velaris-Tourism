Velaris is small and magical enough that there are no large inns named in canon — guests usually stay with friends or in modest taverns. Wolf's Den Inn is the most famous lodging for travellers (albeit infamously the “worst tavern in Velaris”); it offers basic rooms and hearty Illyrian ale — a rustic experience for adventurous souls. Wealthy guests, ambassadors, or Inner Circle friends might be invited to private homes: the elegant Town House has guest quarters for visiting officials, and Feyre and Rhysand's riverside River House (a sprawling manor) could, in theory, host family — though these are not open to the public. In practice, travellers seek rooms in small guesthouses or stay as guests of Velaris families. Magical wards mean you'll need an invitation or to be known to someone inside to gain access.
