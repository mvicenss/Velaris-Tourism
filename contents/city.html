Velaris, also known as the Court of Dreams, is a hidden faerie city nestled against the Sidra River and flanked by towering red mountains.

Visitors step into a vibrant, artist-friendly haven sculpted of white marble and warm sandstone. The cityscape is defined by:

  1. Rolling hills and panoramic river views
  2. Pastel-painted neighbourhoods
  3. A canopy of brilliant stars

While long kept secret by powerful enchantments, today Velaris dazzles travelers with its art, culture, and gentle beauty.
